## What it is

The [Drupal AI Learners Club](https://www.drupal.org/about/ai/initiatives/drupal-ai-learners-club) is a community space for Drupal developers and site builders who want to figure out AI together — without the pressure of being an expert.

Think **Drupal Dojo, but for AI.** We meet regularly to talk about what's new, do show-and-tell demos of handy tools and workflows, and support each other navigating a landscape that changes weekly.

## What we do

Each session typically mixes:

- **What's new** — models, IDE features, agent frameworks, policy shifts
- **Show and tell** — someone demos a workflow that actually saved them time (or spectacularly didn't)
- **Open discussion** — questions, debates, and "has anyone tried…?" moments

The inaugural session drew **97 participants**. Sessions are recorded with recaps and links to everything shared in chat.

Join the conversation in **#ai-learners** on Drupal Slack — that's also where we suggest, vote on, and volunteer for future topics.

## Why it exists

AI stirred up real tension in the Drupal community. Some folks were excited, some skeptical, some worried about job impact and code quality. Rather than let that simmer in side channels, the Club became a neutral place to **learn out loud** — curious, practical, and honest about what works and what doesn't.

Coverage so far includes [The Drop Times](https://www.drupal.org/about/initiatives/ai/drupal-ai-learners-club/blog/drupal-ai-learners-club-coverage-by-the-drop-times), [Talking Drupal #555](https://www.drupal.org/about/initiatives/ai/drupal-ai-learners-club/blog/talking-drupal-555-ai-learners-club), and threads on r/drupal.
